Preserving the environment

Jordan Phosphate Mines Company recognizes the long-term health of our company It cannot be isolated from the health of the environment we manage

Commitment to development

Its commitment to continuous development in the field of occupational safety and health with the aim of:

  • Preventing injuries among workers by ensuring safe working conditions and a suitable environment While adhering to the work permit system and providing the necessary safety and protection means
  • Preventing exposure to occupational diseases among workers by identifying and evaluating the risks that arise It leads to the occurrence of occupational diseases and the gradual application of the necessary control measures starting from Eliminate dangers and end up using safety equipment as a last resort. In addition, you do The company conducts periodic medical examinations for all employees within its facilities.
  • Educating workers about the risks related to occupational safety and health and training them on means of control on these risks
  • Educating and training workers on the use of personal protective equipment and firefighting methods
  • Apply approved work procedures in the field of operation, maintenance and all other activities accordingly Documented systems in the company.
  • Commitment to following up on developments in the subject of occupational safety and health to ensure the development of the system And improve performance.
  • The company is committed to informing contractors of the occupational safety and health policy approved in the complex Industrial in order to adhere to its content
