Preserving the environment

Jordan Phosphate Mines Company recognizes the long-term health of our company It cannot be isolated from the health of the environment we manage

Our commitment to preserving the environment

As a global company, we are committed to reducing the environmental impacts of our business, products and care Citizens and natural resources in the Kingdom.

Protecting the environment is one of the mission objectives that the company seeks to achieve in accordance with the standards set for it Local and international, the company seeks to invest in the world's advanced technological development Work within this course is to preserve the safety of the environment and reduce water and energy consumption.
The company is keen on the environment and public safety, and that its activities are carried out within environmental conditions Sound through the application of environmental, social and occupational health systems, in line with standards Internationally approved to preserve the various environmental elements within the company’s sites and in the surrounding areas These sites preserve and use natural resources, including surface and groundwater, etc Optimization of these resources and the preservation of the distinctive marine environment in the Gulf of Aqaba region.
