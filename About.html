Jordan Phosphate Company

The Jordan Phosphate Mines Company is a Jordanian public joint stock limited company established in 1949 Its current capital is 247.5 million dinars, aiming to exploit phosphate ores in Jordan.

During the past seven decades, the company has assumed a leading position among international companies in the fields of exploration The fertilizer industry, which is a major component of the structure of the Jordanian economy and its exports.
The activities of the Jordan Phosphate Mines Company fall within two integrated sectors: the mining sector and the mining sector Manufacture of phosphate fertilizers, and through the integration of the two sectors, the company has strongly proven its worth within Global markets.
The company carries out its production activities in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, which has the fifth largest reserve Phosphate in the world at 3.7 billion tons, of which 1.250 billion tons are the company’s mine reserves, to be a company Jordanian phosphate mines are the first exporter and fifth largest producer of phosphate in the world with a production capacity of more than 100% About 11 million tons of phosphate annually.
The company's main headquarters is located in the capital, Amman. The company also owns three mines, located in the center and south Kingdom,
These are the Hasa, Wadi Al-Abyad and Al-Shidiya mines, in addition to the industrial complex in the city of Aqaba, which aims to convert phosphate ore into products and increase their added value. The industrial complex is one of the largest phosphate fertilizer complexes in the Middle East. In addition, the company has built the phosphate port located in the city of Aqaba, which specializes in exporting phosphate, and will enhance phosphate export operations to various global markets. It is also currently expanding and developing the industrial port and adding amendments that take environmental issues into account. Today, we are proud of our achievements, products, and our fulfillment of our obligations towards our customers, the rights and gains of our employees, and the provision of more and more job opportunities for the sons of the homeland, and the establishment of a solid series of successful projects with strategic partners in international markets and domestically to produce phosphate ore, expand exploration areas and manufacture phosphate fertilizers of all kinds. To maintain this leadership, we are working hard to improve performance, develop products and raise their competitive quality.

Our Vision

At the Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company, we believe that the most important sources of our success are our employees 2133 employees and 167 trainees, and our investment in them is the most important investment for the company in order to ensure... Progress and achieving its goals aimed at increasing production and meeting the needs of the global market.

Our Message

Leadership, excellence and sustainability in the mining and phosphate fertilizers sector within the highest standards of quality and environmental conservation.

Our Mission

The Phosphate Company carries out phosphate exploration and mining operations, and develops innovative products with high-quality specifications and global competition by employing modern technology, training and qualifying its human cadres, taking into account all aspects of sustainable development of its operations on the environment and society.

Our core values

Responsibility towards society and the environment The company focuses on achieving the highest standards of quality and sustainability, while promoting innovation in production and development.
