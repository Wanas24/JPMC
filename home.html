Asia

The Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company exports phosphate and related products to different countries all over the world across the Asian continent. As one of the leading global suppliers, the company has strong business relationships with... Many countries in Asia, including India, which is one of their major markets for phosphate materials Raw. The company's focus on expanding into international markets helps maintain its economic growth And its global influence in the phosphate industry.
These exports include raw phosphate and manufactured fertilisers, which contribute significantly to the agricultural industries In the region. The company's strategy includes expansion into other Asian markets.
