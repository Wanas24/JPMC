Board of Directors

Jordan Phosphate Mines Company is a Jordanian public shareholding company, managed by a Board of Directors responsible for determining the company's strategies and directions and supervising its affairs.

Private sector representatives

Chairman of the Board of Directors: His Excellency Dr. Mohammed Al-Dhneibat

His Excellency Dr. Mohammad Mahmoud Al-Dhneibat assumed the chairmanship of the Board of Directors of the Jordan Phosphate Mines Company as of 3/28/2017, representing the private sector.
  • Currently holds the position of Chairman of the Board of Trustees of Al-Hussein Bin Talal University. Currently holds the position of Chairman of the Board of Trustees of Al-Hussein Bin Talal University.

Top management
CEO/General Manager

Dear Engineer Abdul Wahab Al-Rawad

Engineer Abdul Wahab Al-Rawad assumed his position as CEO/General Manager of the company Jordanian phosphate mines on November 18, 2019.

  • He was born in Ma'an, Jordan, on September 1, 1963.
  • Chemical engineer, graduate of the University of Jordan in 1986.
  • He began his career in 1989 in the industrial complex, one of the production sites Affiliated to the Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company.
  • He was seconded in 1995 to work at the Indo-Jordanian Chemical Company - one of the... The wholly owned subsidiary of the Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company.
  • In 2017, he was promoted to the position of General Manager of the Indo-Jordan Chemical Company. And it has Khalarqi was appointed in 2017 to the position of General Manager of the Indo-Jordan Chemical Company During his tenure, he demonstrated that he excelled in taking the company to advanced levels and achieving the highest profits In the company's history compared to previous years.
