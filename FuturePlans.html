Planning and strategic thinking

Strategic planning and thinking are the most important factors for the continued success of Jordan Phosphate Mines Company.

Future plans aim to reduce costs, increase production capacity, diversify exports, develop infrastructure and asset sustainability, support economic, environmental and social development, improve customer experience, enhance core values, develop human resources and improve institutional performance.
The company works to raise and improve the quality of products in existing industrial facilities, by completing the modernization of the company's factories in all locations, increasing their production capacity, and modifying manufacturing methods to suit the requirements of environmental conservation.
As part of the company's plans to increase its production, diversify its exports of final and intermediate products of phosphate, fertilizers, phosphoric acid, aluminum fluoride, and provide the needs of joint projects and other industrial facilities for water, the company has drilled (6) deep wells in the Al-Shidiya area with a production capacity of 10 million cubic meters annually, in addition to the company's intention to drill (5) new wells in the Al-Jafr area with a production capacity of 4 million cubic meters annually, in addition to other facilities represented by the water network, a hot water cooling tower, the necessary pumping stations, control systems and supplying the project with electrical energy, in addition to the necessary road network, to cover the company's current and future needs.
The Phosphate Mines Company owns a seaport for exporting phosphate, in implementation of the development and operation agreement concluded with the Aqaba Development Company / Aqaba Special Economic Zone Authority. The phosphate export port is located in the industrial zone on the southern coast of Aqaba, where the handling volume in the port is 6 million tons annually, which is designed to berth various ships with loads of (5 - 100) thousand tons. In addition, the Phosphate Mines Company owns 50% of the private joint-stock industrial ports company represented by both the Jordan Phosphate Mines Company and the Arab Potash Company according to the agreement concluded between the two companies, where this company exports products from DAP fertilizers, potash, and concentrated phosphoric acid, and imports its needs of raw materials necessary for the manufacture of fertilizers and their various derivatives such as ammonia and sulfur, in addition to meeting the needs of allied and affiliated companies.
Within the framework of the company's strategic plan aimed at increasing production capacity, diversifying final and intermediate products of fertilizers, vertically and integrated with mining operations, to increase the added value of the company's products and give the company sufficient flexibility to deal with market fluctuations, it continued to proceed according to the plan set to implement several projects to produce phosphoric acid and aluminum fluoride and raise the percentage of phosphate through flotation.
