Phosphate obtains the Jordanian Quality Mark for its products

Amman 09-17-2024
The Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company obtained the Jordanian Quality Mark, awarded by the Jordanian Standards and Metrology Institution, for its aluminum fluoride products. And diammonium phosphate (DAP) fertilizer.
This came after the company completed all requirements for granting the Jordanian Quality Mark to its products according to the Jordanian Quality Mark instructions No. 3/2016 and its technical requirements No. 124/2024. This allows the company to place the Jordanian Quality Mark logo on these products.
The Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Jordan Phosphate Mines Company, Dr. Muhammad Al-Thanibat, congratulated the company’s executive management and all its employees on this great achievement, praising the efforts made. Efforts were made in this context and enabled the company to obtain this distinctive mark.
According to Dr. Al-Thanibat, this comes as a translation of the efforts made by the company with the aim of rooting a culture of excellence and quality in the production process that keeps pace with developments and the requirements of quality standards. Local and international, stressing in this context that the company will continue to develop innovative products with high quality specifications and compete at the local and global levels by employing Modern technology.
Dr. Al-Thanibat said that this achievement is added to the company’s record of achievements and excellence, and the local, regional and international awards and certificates it has obtained in the fields of Quality, occupational health, safety and environment, risk management and applied scientific research, and the R4E Recognition of Excellence Certificate  awarded by the King Abdullah II Center for Excellence.
Dr. Al-Thanibat praised the role of the Standards and Metrology Organization in stimulating distinguished industrial companies, in a way that enhances the role, success and competitiveness of national institutions, and in a way that constitutes An incentive to continue the path of giving and excellence. To provide the best products and services to customers, and enhance its ability and global competitive position.
In turn, the CEO of the company, Engineer Abdul Wahab Al-Rawad, said that the company obtained the Jordanian Quality Mark for its aluminum fluoride and diphosphate fertilizer products. Ammonium DAP reflects its efforts to provide high-quality and effective products, and enhances customer confidence in its products and the company’s competitiveness locally and globally.
Engineer Al-Rowad said that granting this mark represents the efforts made by the company within the framework of its development plans, to shift towards manufacturing industries and achieve the highest quality standards. Global production efficiency and competition.
The Directorate of Conformity Certificates at the Jordanian Standards and Metrology Institution congratulated the Jordanian Phosphate Mines Company for obtaining the Jordanian Quality Mark for its products, praising This achievement adds to the company's record of accumulated successes in various fields.
Granting the Jordanian Quality Mark comes within the framework of the efforts undertaken by the Jordanian Standards and Metrology Institution to enhance confidence in the quality of national products and support the competitiveness of the economy. Jordanian according to best practices.
Obtaining the quality mark is a government service for the Jordanian industrial sector. With the aim of raising the quality of its products and increasing its ability to compete in local and foreign markets, which allows The opportunity for manufacturers to constantly keep pace with changes in industries, enhance their reputation and enable them to market their products as high-quality products.
