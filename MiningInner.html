Al Sheidiya Mine

  1. Home

  3. Al Sheidiya Mine

Productivity of 7 million tons annually

It is located 80 km southeast of the city of Ma'an, and its production capacity is approximately seven Millions of tons per year and consists of several layers; There are three layers A1, A2, A3, Average phosphate content (Tris Calcium Phosphate- TCP)

Al-Sheidiya mine is located 50 km southeast of the city of Ma'an, within an area of Approximately 315 square kilometers.

Because the region is rich in phosphates, the Phosphate Mining Company conducted a detailed study of Al-Sheidiya During the period 1975-1979, studies and excavations continued according to the following chronological sequence:
  • From 1977-1987, 173 wells were manually drilled with a diameter of 1.25 m, for the purposes of further verification and follow-up. Material extension
  • From 1987-2002, a total of 4200 wells were drilled within drilling networks (50*30) and (100*60) m. In the first and second mines for quality control purposes. 200 wells were drilled with a network (400*400). In the upper part of the second mine, for the purposes of checking positions and extension Raw materials. 55 manual wells with a diameter of 1.25 m were also added.
  • From 1997-2002, a total of 2,600 wells were drilled in the upper crude area, in addition to 6 trial trenches
  • In the period from 1997 to 2002, a total of 2,600 wells were drilled in the upper crude oil region, in addition to 6 trial trenches

Shedi raw materials are classified into the following classes:

  • Layer A1 and its three levels (D, C, B) are mined, as it requires washing operations and raising the percentage of
  • A2 layer is a phosphate with a high percentage of tricalcium phosphate, which does not require more From the carburizing process
  • Layer A3 which is sandy phosphate and phosphate sand. The soft parts are mined and the hardened parts are left in place and this type is subjected to operations Washing and flotation. In the upper ore zone, raw materials are classified into the following classes:
    • S1: It requires washing and raising the percentage
    • S2: It is a phosphate that contains a high percentage of tricalcium phosphate. It does not require For more than the carburizing process.
